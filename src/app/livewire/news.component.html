Grassroot LiveWire

LiveWire is a service that enables Grassroot users to generate and issue alerts on events occurring in their community, and have those be automatically composed (after human review) and sent as press releases to news desks. All alerts are listed here, forming a news feed of ground-level activity in South Africa's marginalized communities.
