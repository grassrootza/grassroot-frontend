Set up a Grassroot Extra account

Grassroot is free for groups of up to 300 people and 10 meetings, votes and actions per month. For larger and more active groups, and to run campaigns or create movements, you will need to set up a Grassroot Extra account. An account will unlock larger groups, more frequent activity, campaigns, movements, and a wide range of other features.

Grassroot Extra costs R300 per month, including 1,000 free SMSs per month, and 25c per SMS (including VAT) for each one ahead. You can either pay for the first month with a credit card now and enable the account immediately, or create the account and pay by ETF, sending the proof of payment to accounts@grassroot.org.za.
